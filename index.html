An appeal from owners of Navin's Starwood Towers

We, the owners of Navin's Starwood Towers - Phase I, Medavakkam, Chennai have come together in our angst and created this web page to highlight how Navin Housing and Properties (P) Ltd - the builder have repeatedly ignored our rights and sidelined our reasonable concerns for their commercial interests. The following issues have been repeatedly raised to Navin's over the past 4 years- ever since they had completed the construction of Phase I, individually by owners and by the elected Associations.

  • Exploitation over Clubhouse - Lily of the Valley

    Navin's has collected 1.5 lakh towards Clubhouse membership and a premium rate per sft than neighbouring developers from every owner, stating the worldclass amenities in offer there. It took them over 4 years to complete the construction and public opening of this Clubhouse. Now, they demand a minimum yearly subscription of INR 10,000 from each family to use even the basic amenities provided. This is over the very high society maintenance of INR 3.5/Sft that we are being charged by them. Our repeated requests to have basic facilities like Gym, Swimming Pool and Childrens Play area free of charge is being ignored.

  • Unmet OSR Requirements

    Navin's has not cleared the OSR area required for such a development and are using the area allotted for storing their heavy construction equipments, free of charge.
